what i'm doing now

this is a now page. i use this to motivate myself and show you what i'm currently focused on.
i was inspired by others.

  • 1 - Home Routine

    I'm currently back in my hometown of Minnetonka, MN with my family. I've been taking time to reflect on my college experience; like many, I made wonderful friends who molded me into who I am today. There's a lot of sorrow in leaving close ones, but I'm excited for what's to come, for them and myself. I'm biking nearly every day and working on personal projects like this!

  • 2 - Personal Website

    I'm spending a lot of time building this site! I decided I'd like to strengthen my online presence. A personal site will act as a centralized hub for my projects and social media as well as a more personal spot to express myself.

  • 3 - Work Preparation

    I start full-time at William Blair in Chicago as an Investment Banking Analyst in July. I'm spending time studying for the SIE Exam which I hope to take in the next couple of weeks.

  • 4 - Book Notes

    One of the most important things that I've learned in recent years is the power of reflection. In a world with so much content to consume, most of it goes in one ear and out the other. The most powerful technique I've found to retain important information is deliberate reflection. That is why I'll be spending time every week for the foreseeable future reflecting on and condensing the notes I've taken the books I read during college. I hope to keep a permanent database of my notes on this site for myself and anyone who cares to read.

  • 5 - 30 Day Challenges

    All skills in life take time to develop. Many of them take so much time that progress is difficult to see. Countless people, myself included, struggle to stay motivated when learning new things because the change is so slow. I've become particularly fond of YouTubers doing something along the lines of a "30 Day Challenge" to show the immense effects of consistent, incremental progress. That is why I'm currently planning my own 30 Day Challenges that I will document in short YouTube videos. I'll pick a skill each month to practice each day. My hope is that documentation of my progress will act as a form of motivation for myself and others.
